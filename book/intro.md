# Welcome to the Data Literacy Handbook

_This handbook is a work in progress, you can check the status of our progress in the {ref}`Roadmap page. <roadmap>`_

If you would like to contribute to the project please [get in touch with us -link to maastricht university lirbrary?- or use the review option above or branch in GitHub?]

The Data Literacy Handbook collects knowledge and resources for students and teachers. In this handbook you will find: 

1. An introduction to different concepts and aspects of Data Literacy
2. Exercises to get familiar and improve one's data literacy skills
3. Templates for processes, courses, etc.
